Future of the Farm

Our Vision

Our ultimate goal is to create a fully self-sustaining ecosystem that not only provides healthy, nutrient-dense food for our community but also serves as a model for regenerative practices in Utah.

In the coming years, we plan to expand our high tunnel capacity, diversify our pollinator forage even further, and implement more advanced water conservation techniques. We want to be a place where the community can learn about the importance of soil health and the 17 essential nutrients.

Building Connections

As our logo suggests, we thrive on building a web of connections between customers, farmers, and the soil. Our future involves strengthening these bonds through community-supported agriculture and educational workshops.

We believe that by healing the soil, we heal ourselves and our planet. Thank you for being part of this journey with us.
