What We Grow

Every season, we carefully select varieties that thrive in our local climate and provide the highest nutrient density and flavor. Here is a look at what we have planned for this year.

Potatoes

We grow several varieties, including the striking Purple Viking potatoes, known for their creamy texture and vibrant color.

Garlic

Our garlic is grown with care, ensuring robust flavor and excellent storage quality for our customers.

Peppers

From sweet to spicy, our peppers are a staple of our summer harvest, grown in our protected high tunnels.

Produce Mix

In addition to our staples, we grow a variety of seasonal produce focused on what our community loves most.
