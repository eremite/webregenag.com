Beekeeping at Web Regen

Bees are the heartbeat of our farm. By eliminating "icides" and planting diverse forage, we create a sanctuary for honeybees and native pollinators.

Our beekeeping practices focus on the health and well-being of the colony. We see the bees as essential partners in our regenerative journey, ensuring the pollination of our crops and the wild Utah flora that surrounds us.

Natural Honeycomb

We allow our bees to build natural comb, respecting their natural biological processes.

Pollinator Corridors

Our farm is designed with "pollinator corridors" to provide year-round forage for our bees.

Sweet Connections

The honey we harvest is a reflection of the diverse wild and cultivated flowers on our land.
