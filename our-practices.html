Our Practices

Regenerative agriculture isn't just about "doing less harm." It's about actively healing the ecosystem. By focusing on soil health, we unlock a chain reaction of benefits. We reject the monoculture model and instead integrate a wide variety of plant species to build a farm that is naturally resistant to pests and climate fluctuations.

Our techniques focus on building soil organic matter and restoring degraded soil biodiversity. This results in both carbon drawdown and improving the water cycle. We don't use "-icides" (pesticides, herbicides, fungicides) because we believe in working with nature, not against it.

Biodiversity

We plant a diverse range of crops to ensure a healthy, resilient ecosystem that supports various forms of life.

Season Extension

Using high tunnels allows us to protect our crops and extend our growing season in the Summit, UT climate.

Land Stewardship

We view ourselves as stewards of the land, working to leave the soil healthier than we found it.
