Our Practices

Healthy, productive land starts with a commitment to soil health through four foundational practices. By maintaining constant soil coverage with mulch or cover crops and keeping living roots in the ground year-round, we protect the earth from erosion and feed the vital microbial networks below. We pair this with minimal soil disturbance to preserve the soil's natural structure and integrate a wide diversity of crops to break pest cycles and enhance nutrient cycling. Together, these methods build resilient ecosystems that capture carbon, conserve water, and grow nutrient-dense food for the long haul.
